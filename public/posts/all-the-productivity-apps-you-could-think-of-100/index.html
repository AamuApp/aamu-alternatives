    All the productivity apps you could think of (100+)

    June 12, 2025 · Updated June 12, 2025 · 7 min · Ilkka Huotari

    Even though we know that Aamu.app is the perfect productivity app, we may be interested to know what else is out there. This is why I’ve compiled this handy list to show you that you don’t actually miss out on anything important, if you choose Aamu.app.

    There are actually tens of thousands of these apps, so no sane person (or even an AI) will ever be able to try them all. But here’s the first hundred and then some.

    Task Management & To-Do Lists

    1. Todoist - Intuitive task management with priority settings and integrations.

    2. TickTick - Combines to-do lists, habit tracking, and calendar views.

    3. Microsoft To Do - Task management integrated with Microsoft 365.

    4. Any.do - Simple task and calendar app with cross-platform sync.

    5. Trello - Visual task management with Kanban boards and automation via Butler.

    6. ClickUp - All-in-one platform for tasks, docs, goals, and time tracking.

    7. Asana - Project and task management with multiple views (lists, boards, timelines).

    8. Monday.com - Customizable work management with automation and integrations.

    9. Wunderlist - Simple to-do list app (now largely replaced by Microsoft To Do).

    10. Taskade - Collaborative task lists with real-time syncing and templates.

    11. OmniFocus - Advanced task management for Apple users with robust prioritization.

    12. Things 3 - Sleek task manager for Apple devices with a focus on simplicity.

    13. MeisterTask - Kanban-style task management with team collaboration.

    14. Todo List - Basic task management (generic, often confused with Todoist).

    15. Weekdone - OKR and goal-tracking tool with progress analytics.

    16. Wrike - Enterprise-grade task and project management with reporting.

    17. Smartsheet - Spreadsheet-based project management with automation.

    18. Airtable - Flexible database and task management with customizable templates.

    19. Basecamp - All-in-one project management with to-do lists and messaging.

    20. ProofHub - Project management with task tracking and team collaboration.

    21. Jira Software - Agile project management for software development teams.

    22. GanttPRO - Gantt chart-based project management for timeline tracking.

    23. Teamwork - Project management for small to midsize teams with client access.

    24. Favro - Agile project management with card-based workflows.

    25. Quip - Task management with integrated docs and spreadsheets.

    26. Flow - Task and project management with a focus on simplicity.

    27. Hive - Collaborative project management with analytics and integrations.

    28. Notion - Customizable workspace for tasks, notes, and databases.

    29. Coda - Doc-based task management with interactive workflows.

    30. ClickUp Brain - AI-powered task summarization and automation within ClickUp.

    31. Linear - Lightweight issue tracking and project management for software teams.

    32. Plane - Open-source alternative to Jira and Linear with issue tracking and roadmaps.

    Time Tracking & Scheduling

    1. Toggl Track - Simple time tracking with integrations for project management.

    2. Harvest - Time tracking with invoicing and project budget tools.

    3. Clockify - Free time tracking with calendar views and team reports.

    4. Hubstaff - Time tracking with employee monitoring and payroll integration.

    5. Everhour - Time tracking with project management integrations.

    6. RescueTime - Automated time tracking for productivity insights.

    7. WebWork Time Tracker - Time and productivity monitoring with screenshots.

    8. HourStack - Time tracking with calendar and project integrations.

    9. Motion - AI-powered time management and scheduling for teams.

    10. Calendly - Automated scheduling for meetings and appointments.

    11. Doodle - Group scheduling and polling for meeting coordination.

    12. SavvyCal - Modern scheduling with personalized links and branding.

    13. TimeCamp - Time tracking with automatic task categorization.

    14. Timely - AI-driven time tracking with memory-based logging.

    15. Financial Cents - Time tracking and project management for accounting.

    16. Amie - Time management and note-taking app with scheduling integration.

    Communication & Collaboration

    1. Slack - Team messaging with channels and app integrations.

    2. Microsoft Teams - Collaboration hub with chat, video, and Office 365 integration.

    3. Zoom - Video conferencing with screen sharing and recording.

    4. Google Meet - Video conferencing integrated with Google Workspace.

    5. Skype - Video and voice calls with messaging and screen sharing.

    6. Beeper - Unified messaging app for multiple platforms.

    7. Fleep - Messaging app with email integration and file sharing.

    8. Zoho Cliq - Team chat with task and calendar integration.

    9. RingCentral - VoIP platform with phone, video, and messaging.

    10. Dialpad - AI-powered VoIP and video conferencing solution.

    11. Krisp - AI noise-canceling app for clear calls and recordings.

    12. Fellow - Meeting management with notes and action item tracking.

    13. Loom - Screen recording with AI transcription and editing.

    14. Clariti - AI-powered hub for email, chat, and docs.

    15. Discord - Community-driven messaging with voice and video.

    Note-Taking & Documentation

    1. Evernote - Note-taking with search and organization features.

    2. Google Docs - Collaborative document editing with real-time sync.

    3. Microsoft OneNote - Note-taking integrated with Microsoft 365.

    4. Notion AI - AI-enhanced note-taking within the Notion platform.

    5. Obsidian - Knowledge base with markdown-based note-linking.

    6. Roam Research - Note-taking with networked thought organization.

    7. Bear - Minimalist note-taking for Apple users with markdown support.

    8. Joplin - Open-source note-taking with cross-platform sync.

    9. Tettra - Knowledge base for team documentation and wikis.

    10. Guru - Knowledge management with AI search and integrations.

    11. Confluence - Team documentation with Jira and Atlassian integration.

    12. Coda - Interactive docs with task management features.

    13. ClickUp Docs - Documentation tool within the ClickUp ecosystem.

    14. Dropbox Paper - Collaborative document editing with file storage.

    15. Slite - Team documentation with a focus on simplicity.

    Automation & Workflow

    1. Zapier - Automates workflows by connecting apps with "Zaps."

    2. IFTTT - Automation for apps, devices, and smart home systems.
